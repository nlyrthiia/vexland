# How to Play VexLand: A Complete Guide

Welcome to VexLand, a Web3 roguelike dungeon crawler on Avalanche! This guide will help you get started, even if you're new to blockchain gaming.

## 🌟 What is VexLand?

VexLand is a Web3 roguelike game that combines traditional dungeon crawling with blockchain technology. Your progress, achievements, and rewards are recorded on the Avalanche blockchain, making them truly yours.

### Key Features:

- **🎮 Classic Roguelike Gameplay**: Explore dangerous dungeons, battle monsters, and discover treasures
- **⛓️ Blockchain Integration**: All progress saved on Avalanche
- **💎 Real Rewards**: Earn points, diamonds, and exclusive items
- **🏆 Daily Challenges**: Check in daily for rewards and compete on leaderboards
- **🛍️ In-Game Store**: Purchase Season Pass and exclusive items
- **📱 Cross-Platform**: Play on PC, mobile, and tablet

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

You'll need a EVM-compatible wallet to play.

#### Avalanche Wallet (Recommended)

1. Visit [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)
2. Install the browser extension for Chrome/Firefox
3. Create a new wallet or import existing
4. **Important**: Save your recovery phrase safely!

Other supported wallets: Core Wallet, Rabby

### Step 2: Get Some AVAX Tokens

You'll need AVAX tokens for the Season Pass and gas fees:

1. **Centralized Exchanges**: Buy AVAX on Binance, OKX, or Bybit, then withdraw to your EVM wallet address
2. **Bridge from Ethereum**: Use Wormhole to bridge assets to Avalanche
3. **Testnet Faucet** (for testing): Request free testnet AVAX from the Avalanche faucet

#### Recommended Amount:

- **Minimum**: 2 AVAX (Season Pass + gas)
- **Comfortable**: 5 AVAX for the full experience

### Step 3: Connect and Play

1. Visit [vexland.xyz](https://vexland.xyz)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" or "GET STARTED"
3. Select your EVM wallet
4. Approve the connection
5. Start playing!

## 🎮 How to Play

### Basic Controls

#### Desktop:

- **W, A, S, D**: Move your character
- **Mouse**: Interact with objects and UI
- **Spacebar**: Attack/Use items

#### Mobile:

- **Long-press**: Reveal virtual joystick for movement
- **Tap**: Interact with objects
- **Swipe**: Navigate menus

### Game Mechanics

#### Daily Check-in System:

- **Check in daily** to earn points and rewards
- **Season Pass holders** get 3x points bonus!
- **Cooldown period**: 24 hours between check-ins
- **Leaderboard**: Compete with other players in your area

#### Points and Rewards:

- **Points**: Earned through daily check-ins and gameplay
- **Diamonds**: Rare currency for special items
- **Experience**: Track your progress and unlock features

#### Achievements System:

- **Points Master**: Accumulate 1,000 points
- **Experience Collector**: Reach 50,000 experience
- **Loyal Player**: Check in 15 times

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

#### Wallet Connection Problems:

- Refresh the page and try again
- Make sure your wallet extension is unlocked
- Check if you're on the correct network (Avalanche Fuji Testnet)

#### Transaction Failures:

- Ensure you have enough AVAX for gas fees
- Check your internet connection

#### Game Loading Issues:

- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
